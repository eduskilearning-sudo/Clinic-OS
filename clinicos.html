How it works

Live in three steps

No installation. No IT vendor. No demo calls. Just sign up, share your link, and you're live.

01
Create your free clinic account

Enter your name, clinic, and specialty. ClinicOS instantly generates your custom booking page at clinicos.in/your-clinic-name.

02
Share your booking link

Paste it in your WhatsApp Status, Google Maps listing, or clinic notice board. Patients self-book — no phone calls needed.

03
Run your queue from anywhere

Watch the live dashboard on your phone or tablet. Move patients through Confirmed → Waiting → Done with a single tap.

What's included

Everything your clinic needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Built for Indian clinics: low bandwidth, multi-device, multilingual-ready, and WhatsApp-native by design.

Custom Booking Link

Your clinic gets a permanent, shareable URL at clinicos.in/your-name. Patients book 24/7 without calling.

Live Tri-State Queue

Every patient moves through Confirmed → Waiting → Done in real time. Syncs instantly across all staff devices.

Digital Patient Records

A searchable EMR indexed by name or phone number. Full visit history, notes, and complaints in one place.

WhatsApp Automation

Booking confirmations and "You're next!" proximity alerts sent automatically to every patient's WhatsApp.

Role-Based Access

Doctor sees everything. Receptionist manages check-ins and the queue. Financial data stays protected by role.

Revenue Analytics

Auto-calculated weekly and monthly revenue summaries, busiest slots, and consultation trends. No spreadsheets.
